## Flex

Flex is the shorthand for `flex-grow`, `flex-shrink` and `flex-basis` combined. 

Use `.flex-auto` to allow a flex item to grow and shrink in accordance to it's initial size.

<Sandbox>
  <Sandbox.Preview>
  <span className="c-black fs-caption-2 my-2 d-block">Default behaviour</span>
    <div className="flex bg-tertiary-100 w-100p">
        <div className="c-black bg-tertiary-400 px-4 py-2 m-2 ta-center">Short</div>
        <div className="c-black bg-tertiary-400 px-4 py-2 m-2 ta-center">Medium length</div>
        <div className="c-black bg-tertiary-400 px-4 py-2 m-2 ta-center">Larger in length when compared</div>
    </div>
    <span className="c-black fs-caption-2 my-2 d-block">Flex auto</span>
    <div className="flex bg-tertiary-100 w-100p">
        <div className="flex-auto c-black bg-tertiary-400 px-4 py-2 m-2 ta-center">Short</div>
        <div className="flex-auto c-black bg-tertiary-400 px-4 py-2 m-2 ta-center">Medium length</div>
        <div className="flex-auto c-black bg-tertiary-400 px-4 py-2 m-2 ta-center">Larger in length when compared</div>
    </div>
  </Sandbox.Preview>
  <Sandbox.Code>
    <div className="flex">
        <div className="flex-auto">...</div>

</Sandbox>
