## Text color
Text colour classes take on the syntax of `.c-{palette}-{shade}`.

<Sandbox>
  <Sandbox.Preview>
    <p className="c-secondary-500 m-0">
      The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog
    </p>
  </Sandbox.Preview>
  <Sandbox.Code>
    <p className="c-secondary-500">...</p>
  </Sandbox.Code>
</Sandbox>
<Spacer my={6}/>



## Background colour
Similar to text colours, background classes take on the syntax of `.bg-{palette}-{shade}`.

<Sandbox>
  <Sandbox.Preview>
    <button
      className="button bs-solid bw-1 br-4 lh-solid box-border h-9 fs-body-2 px-4 py-2 bg-primary-500 c-white c-pointer bc-transparent"
    >
      Button
    </button>
  </Sandbox.Preview>
  <Sandbox.Code>
    <button className="bg-primary-500">...</button>
  </Sandbox.Code>
</Sandbox>
<Spacer my={24}/>

## Hoverable
All color classes support a `hover:` modifier. The syntax is `.hover:bg-{palette}-{shade}`.

<Sandbox>
  <Sandbox.Preview>
    <p className="c-secondary-500 hover:c-primary-500 m-0 c-pointer">
      The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog
    </p>
  </Sandbox.Preview>
  <Spacer my={2} />
  <Sandbox.Preview>
    <button
      className="button bc-transparent br-4 lh-solid box-border h-9 fs-body-2 px-4 py-2 bg-transparent c-white bg-primary-500 hover:bg-primary-600 c-pointer"
    >
      Button
    </button>
  </Sandbox.Preview>
  <Spacer my={2} />
  <Sandbox.Preview>
      <button
        className="button bs-solid bw-1 br-4 lh-solid box-border h-9 fs-body-2 px-4 py-2 bg-transparent c-primary-500 hover:c-primary-800 c-pointer bc-primary-500 hover:bc-primary-800"
      >
        Button

  </Sandbox.Preview>
  <Sandbox.Code>
